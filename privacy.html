Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-25

Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Lomami Ventures (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information in connection with our website, communications, scheduling, consultations, and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

Confidentiality and discretion

We endeavor to treat communications, appointments, and consultations as private and confidential to the extent reasonably practicable and consistent with applicable law, operational necessity, and the terms below. We do not sell personal information to data brokers.

Information we collect

  • Contact information (e.g., name, email, phone number) that you provide.
  • Scheduling and service information (e.g., appointment requests, preferences, communications).
  • Payment information (processed by payment providers; we do not store full card numbers).
  • Verification information where required for risk management and compliance (see “Verification”).
  • Technical data (e.g., basic logs, device/browser information) as necessary to operate and secure the Services.

SMS/text messaging (opt-in)

If you opt in to text notifications, you consent to receive transactional and/or informational messages at the number provided. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, reply HELP or email accounts@lomamiventures.com.

Verification and background screening

For safety, fraud prevention, and compliance purposes, we may require identity and/or account verification and may use third-party data sources and screening tools (including services such as BeenVerified and Intelius) to perform risk checks. Such checks may require you to confirm that you are the authorized user of the phone number and payment method provided.

How we use information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Services (including scheduling and communications).
  • To process payments and prevent fraud/unauthorized transactions.
  • To send administrative and service-related messages (including SMS if you opt in).
  • To comply with law, enforce policies, and protect rights, safety, and security.

How we share information

We share information only as reasonably necessary to operate the Services, including with:

  • Service providers (e.g., communications/SMS providers such as Twilio, payment processors, hosting/security vendors).
  • Verification/screening providers for risk checks as described above.
  • Legal/compliance where required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect rights and safety.

Data security and retention

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure. We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for legitimate business purposes, compliance, dispute resolution, and enforcement.

Your choices

  • Opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP.
  • Request updates/corrections by emailing accounts@lomamiventures.com.
